### Tom Yum
A unique blend of Thai organic herbs; galangal, lemon grass, kaffir lime leaves and red chilli simmered to create an enticing hot and spicy soup. Also served clear.

### Tom Kha
A concoction of fresh Thai spices including lemon grass, kaffir lime leaves, galangal and red chillies boiled together and beautifully balanced by creamy coconut milk.
